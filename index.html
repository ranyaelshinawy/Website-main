About Me

I’m a senior at The University of Texas at Austin, majoring in Journalism with a minor in Statistics & Data Science and a certificate in Programming & Computation. I’m also a bridge student in UT’s Master of Science in Information Technology and Management (MSITM) program, where I’m learning how technology, business, and innovation intersect.


With a background that combines communication, analytics, and programming, I’ve developed a unique ability to translate complex technical ideas into clear, relatable stories. My journalism training taught me how to communicate thoughtfully and effectively, while my technical coursework has given me a solid foundation in data and programming. I hope to use this skill to bridge the gap between people and the technology that impacts their lives.


My academic and professional interests live are in machine learning and artificial intelligence. Specifically, how we can apply these technologies ethically and responsibly to solve real-world problems and make everyday life a little easier.
